About Us

Nancy G. Livaudais CPA LLC provides Bookkeeping Services and Quickbooks Set-up and training to new and growing businesses in all industries as well as personal or individual accounts. Owned and operated by Nancy Livaudas, we are based in Mandeville, Louisiana and service the Greater New Orleans area particularly in the St. Tammany Parish cities of Mandeville, Covington and Madisonville.
